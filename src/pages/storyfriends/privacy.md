# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Commitment

StoryFriends is designed with privacy as our top priority. **We do not store any user data or collect personally identifiable information (PII).**

## What We Do

- **AI Processing**: Conversations are processed using Google's Gemini API to generate responses
- **No Data Storage**: The app does not store chat messages, user data, or any personal information
- **No PII Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Store user data, chat messages, or conversations
- ❌ Collect personal information, analytics, or usage data
- ❌ Use cloud storage or databases
- ❌ Track your behavior or require accounts
- ❌ Collect or store any PIIs

## Data Handling

- **Conversations**: Chat messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing but are not stored by our app
- **No Local Storage**: The app does not persist any user data locally
- **No Server Storage**: We do not maintain any servers or databases that store user information

## AI Service Provider

This app uses Google's Gemini API for AI processing. When you use the app, your conversation messages are sent to Google's Gemini service to generate responses. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information about how Google handles data sent to their services.

## Your Privacy

- The StoryFriends app itself never stores your data
- We do not collect any personally identifiable information
- All conversations are processed in real-time without being saved

---

_Your privacy is our priority. We don't store your data—period._
