# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Commitment

Tulk is designed with privacy as our top priority. **We do not store any user data or collect personally identifiable information (PII).**

## What We Do

- **Translation Processing**: Text you submit is sent to Kagi's translation API to generate translations
- **Anonymous Requests**: API calls are made fully anonymously—no account, device identifiers, or personal information are attached
- **No Data Storage**: The app does not store translations, source text, or any personal information
- **No PII Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Store user data, source text, or translations
- ❌ Collect personal information, analytics, or usage data
- ❌ Log requests or associate them with you
- ❌ Use cloud storage or databases
- ❌ Track your behavior or require accounts
- ❌ Collect or store any PIIs

## Data Handling

- **Translations**: Text is sent to Kagi's translation API for processing but is not stored or logged by our app
- **Anonymous API Calls**: Requests to Kagi contain only the text to translate. No user identity, device info, or other identifying data
- **No Local Storage**: The app does not persist any user data locally
- **No Server Storage**: We do not maintain any servers or databases that store user information

## Third-Party Translation Service

This app uses [Kagi](https://kagi.com)'s translation API to process your text. Requests are made anonymously. We do not attach any identifiers or personal information to those requests. Please refer to [Kagi's Privacy Policy](https://kagi.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://kagi.com/terms) for information about how Kagi handles data sent to their services.

## Your Privacy

- The Tulk app itself never stores your data
- We do not collect any personally identifiable information
- Translation requests are processed in real-time without being saved or logged

---

_Your privacy is our priority. We don't store your data—period._
