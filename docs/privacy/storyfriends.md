# Privacy Policy

## What We Do

- **Local Storage by Default**: Story content, cached narration audio, and app state are stored on your device
- **Selective Cloud Requests**: Story generation and narration requests go through some Google services and send only the content needed to fulfill those features

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Send more data than needed to external AI services
- ❌ Collect personal information, analytics, or usage data for advertising
- ❌ Use your account or purchase data for advertising

## Data Stored Locally

- Storybooks, characters
- App preferences

## Acknowledgements

This app uses Google services for story generation and narration. Generated narration is stored locally after download so repeated playback does not require another request.
